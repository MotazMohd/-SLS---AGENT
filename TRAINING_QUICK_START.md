## Part 5: Create a Job Card (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Convert Appointment to Job

1. Go to **Appointments**
2. Find the appointment you just created
3. Click **Start Job** button

OR

### Step 1b: Create Job Directly

1. Click **Job Cards** in sidebar
2. Click **+ New Job Card**

### Step 2: Fill Job Details

1. **Customer:** John Doe
2. **Vehicle:** Toyota Camry
3. **Service Type:** Oil Change
4. **Description:** Regular oil change service
5. **Assigned To:** Select a technician
6. **Estimated Time:** 1 hour
7. Click **Create Job**

âœ… **Success:** Job card is created with status "Pending"

---

## Part 6: Complete a Service (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Start the Job

1. Go to **Job Cards**
2. Find your job
3. Click **Start Job**
4. Status changes to "In Progress"

### Step 2: Add Parts

1. In the job card, click **Add Parts**
2. Search for "Oil Filter"
3. Select item, enter quantity: 1
4. Click **Add**
5. Repeat for "Engine Oil" - quantity: 4 quarts

### Step 3: Add Labor

1. Click **Add Labor**
2. Enter hours: 1.0
3. Rate: $50/hour (or your rate)
4. Click **Add**

### Step 4: Complete the Job

1. Click **Complete Job**
2. Review total cost
3. Click **Confirm**

âœ… **Success:** Job is completed, invoice is auto-generated
